About Us

Home furniture offers a fine curated collection of contemporary design, home goods and furniture

Created 2011, Home furniture started as a side project of graphic designer Nela Wedam-Liegler and lead to the launch of an international shipping web-store a year after and made Home furniture an insiders tip for an eclectic approach to interior design, rated under the Top 5 Design stores for Design Lovers in Vienna by Forbes Magazin in 2017.

The Home furniture Team also provides their expertise as interior stylists for private and commercial spaces, shoots and film sets.
